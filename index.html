A Letter

My Words to You

Written with every ounce of sincerity I have.

My dearest,

I have been sitting with this for a while now — the weight of words that should never have been said. And I want you to know that I am truly, deeply sorry.

What I said was wrong. Not because of how it was received, but because it was never the truth of how I feel about you. You deserve so much more than a moment of thoughtlessness.

I see you. I see your patience, your kindness, the way you give pieces of yourself so generously. And I failed to honour that in the moment it mattered most.

I cannot undo what was said. But I can choose, every single day from this moment forward, to be the person who deserves to stand beside you.

Please know — you are not just someone I am apologising to. You are someone I want to grow for.

— Always yours 🌸
Made with love & sincerity  •  For you, always
